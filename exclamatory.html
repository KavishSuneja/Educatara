Hurrah!–exclaimed with joy
Alas! Oh!-exclaimed with sorrow,
Wow! What!-exclaimed with surprise
Sorry!–Exclaimed with regret
Well done!-exclaimed with applause,
Pooh!-exclaimed with contempt.


(to remove sign of exclamation(!)exclaimed is used)
For example:
a)Direct:She said,“Oh!I lost my phone.”
Indirect:She exclaimed with sorrow that she had lost her phone.

b)Direct:He said,“Whata beautiful rose!”
Indirect:He exclaimed that the rose was very beautiful.
