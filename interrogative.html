Interrogative Narration

Said & said to - change into ‘asked’ – tense changes Conjunction‘if’ is used with question starting with Helping verb or modals.
For example: is,are, was, were, had, have, has, do, did, will, can, may, could, would etc.

‘If’ is not used with question starting with: what, where, when, which, where, when, who,how.

Note: In direct speech helping verb is followed by subject and in indirect speech subject is followed by helping verb. Question mark (?) is removed in Indirect speech and Full stop (.) is used.
